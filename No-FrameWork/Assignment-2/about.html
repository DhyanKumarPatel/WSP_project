About Us

Dedicated to healing, committed to care.

Our History

Founded in 1950, City General Hospital has served the community for over 75 years. What began as a small clinic has grown into a premier medical center, renowned for its commitment to clinical excellence and patient-centered care. Throughout our history, we have been at the forefront of medical innovation, constantly adapting to meet the evolving needs of our patients.

Our Mission

To improve the health and well-being of the communities we serve through excellence in patient care, education, and research.

Our Vision

To be the trusted leader in healthcare, recognized for delivering high-quality, compassionate care to every patient, every time.
