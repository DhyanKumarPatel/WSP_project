Our Services

Comprehensive healthcare solutions for every stage of life.

Medical Departments

Cardiology

Comprehensive heart care including diagnostics, treatment, and rehabilitation. Our state-of-the-art Cath Lab is available 24/7.

Pediatrics

Specialized care for infants, children, and adolescents. We offer a child-friendly environment to make visits less stressful.

Neurology

Diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the nervous system, including stroke, epilepsy, and Parkinson's disease.

Orthopedics

Treatment for bone, joint, and muscle conditions. We specialize in sports medicine and joint replacement surgeries.

Maternity

Exceptional care for expectant mothers and newborns. Our birthing center offers private suites and advanced monitoring.

Oncology

Advanced cancer care including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and surgical oncology, with a compassionate support team.

Diagnostic Services

Laboratory

Full-service clinical laboratory offering blood work, urinalysis, and pathology services with rapid results.

Radiology / Imaging

X-rays, CT Scans, MRI, and Ultrasound services available. We use low-dose radiation technology for patient safety.

Pharmacy

On-site hospital pharmacy for inpatient needs and prescription pickup upon discharge.
